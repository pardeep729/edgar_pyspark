Explanatory Note

 

This Form 10-K/A amends the Annual Report on Form 10-K of Hewlett-Packard Company for the fiscal year ended October 31, 2009 filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on December 17, 2009 (the “Form 10-K”) for the sole purpose of furnishing in interactive data format Schedule II, “Valuation and Qualifying Accounts,” which was inadvertently omitted from the interactive data submission furnished with the Form 10-K.

 

No other changes have been made to the Form 10-K. This Form 10-K/A speaks as of the original filing date of the Form 10-K, does not reflect events that may have occurred subsequent to the original filing date, and does not modify or update in any way disclosures made in the Form 10-K.
